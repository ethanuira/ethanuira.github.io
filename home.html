Kia Ora Whānau

He uri tenei no Te Tau Ihu ō Te Waka-o-Māui.
Ko te Tapuwae ō Uenuku, ko Tokomaru ōku maunga.
Ko Wairau tōku awa.
Ko Raukawakawa tōku moana.
Ko Tainui, ko Kurahaupo ōku waka.
Ko Ngāti Rarua, ko Ngāti Kuia, ko Rangitāne ōku iwi.
Ko Te Arawaere, ko Paretona, ko Pareteata ōku hapū.
Ko Wairau Pā tōku marae.

With this site I will be updating the look, feel, and functionality regularly until it is at a point of which I am satisfied.

I hope you enjoy my site and I can keep it interesting.
